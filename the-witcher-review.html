Why The Timeline is Confusing?

The biggest problem in Season 1: 3 different timelines running at the same time. Yennefer's story starts 70 years before Geralt's. Ciri's story is only 2 years long. They all meet at the Battle of Sodden Hill at the end.

Once you understand the timeline, the whole show makes sense.

Geralt's Last Wish Explained

In the Djinn episode, Geralt's last wish was: "I wish to bind my fate to Yennefer". This is why they can never stay away from each other. And because of the Law of Surprise, Ciri is also connected to Geralt as his "destiny".

VFX Breakdown - Dragon & Battle Scenes

Netflix spent $80 million on Season 1. The dragon was full CGI, but the castle and armies were real. The Battle of Sodden Hill used 2000+ extras. That's why it looks so real compared to other shows.
